Our Company mission

First Class Office Cleaning opened for business 10 years ago. Founder Amanda Curry set out with the objective of providing first class service to businesses and offices around the Toronto area. A decade later, they have become the well trusted experts of cleaning services.

With a hired team of cleaning experts, we assess your businesses cleaning needs and come up with a solution to meet your requirements. We have the experience and staff to get the job done, and we get it done quickly and efficiently so your business can continue to operate smoothly.

We pride ourselves on making sure your work environment is clean so you can work safely and efficiently.
